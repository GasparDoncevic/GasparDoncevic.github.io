What do I even do?

I really enjoy researching uncommon solutions to problems regarding hardware limitations, pushing the limits of hardware. For me, working on experimental systems and squeezing out the most out of a system is a very satifying process. I create firmware for microcontrolers, design electronics circuits and their PCBs.
My masters thesis, titled Designing a new generation system for High Density surface Electromyography, is a wireless system for detailed measurement of muscle activity with a large electorode array. The modular system was designed to adapt to different sizes of electrode grids. It's currently in the early testing phase and I intend on continuing the systems development.
